# dig-adventure

This game is my attempt at creating a graphical version of my text adventure. The player can move around the screen on a grid and dig for treasure or perform other actions such as lighting a torch, checking their map, or using a metal detector. Every time the player performs an action, including digging, using an inventory item, or walking one step, a monster in the dungeon will also perform an action, moving randomly, taking a break, or moving toward the player. The goal is to dig to find treasure, helpful items, and a key to the exist which the player must also find, while avoiding the monster. If the player lights a torch they can see a wider area (some items, such as lanterns, will light up more of an area). The player will have a circle of light around them that can grow or shrink depending on their lightsource (perhaps they have a candle to begin with). The screen will have a box showing the action, but the bottom of the screen will include text, and either the left or right side of the screen will include a map and inventory.
